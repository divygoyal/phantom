## §FF. 15 viral 2026 AI reel anatomies

### FF.1 Min Choi: "Sora 2 going crazy viral" (10 wild examples)
- **Hook 0-3s**: black title card "SAVING PRIVATE PIKACHU" → handheld combat AI footage → muzzle flash → "MADE 100% WITH SORA 2"
- **Pattern**: "carousel-of-shock" — each 4-second clip is a complete absurdity (Pikachu, Mario, SpongeBob/Breaking Bad, MJ rant, Sam Altman ballet on subway)
- **Caption**: White Helvetica Bold, top-third, single-word emphasis in bright yellow
- **Cuts**: ~11 cuts in 40s = cut every 3.6s
- **Audio**: NO music bed; native AI-generated diegetic audio per clip carries everything
- **CTA**: "Follow @minchoi for daily AI"

- **Steal**: Replicate weekly. Pick model of the week, generate 8-10 clips, hard-cut every 3.6s, yellow Helvetica caption.

### FF.2 Min Choi: "Nano Banana Pro photorealism" (100% AI)

### FF.16 Varun Mayya: AI-news-roundup with multi-celebrity Sora-cameo teaser (decoded May 2026)

The pattern Varun ships weekly. Decoded by frame-by-frame study of his news-roundup reels (e.g. the Robin-Li/Baidu opener that segued through Elon-as-Grok, multi-Sundar-as-Google, Zuck/Meta, etc.). Length ~131s. Vertical 9:16. Avatar locked bottom 40% throughout.

**Structure (timing-precise):**

| Window | Function | Visual |
|---|---|---|
| 0-2s | **Hook 1** — single-celebrity cinematic opener | stylized photo of CEO #1 (Robin Li) with cinematic processing for first 500ms (heavy contrast/desat), brand watermark upper-corner ("Bai du / ERNIE"), captions builds with yellow-highlighted punch word ("China has the **most powerful AI model**") |
| 2-3s | **Stinger** — red caption transition | "NO WAIT!!!" or similar all-caps red text over a quick cut |
| 3-5s | **Hook 2** — second-celebrity cameo teaser | AI-generated frame of Elon Musk with "Grok 4.1 just dropped" caption, similar cinematic processing |
| 5-7s | **Hook 3** — multi-instance Sora-cameo flex | **FOUR Sundar Pichais** in a coordinated row, AI-generated, hands raised at varying gestures, "Google dropped 05 major updates" caption |
| 7-8s | **Stinger** — second red caption transition | "WAIT WHAT!!!" |
| 8s+ | **Numbered countdown body** — 10 items (5-8s each) | each item has its own visual: product UI screen-rec, CGI demo (e.g. Meta SAM 3 segmenting apples), benchmark tables, or app-icon transitions. Numbered card "N — TOPIC NAME" precedes each segment. |
| -3s | Outro | brief CTA / pinned channel handle |

**The hook architecture is multi-layered:**
- **Hook 1** (single celebrity, anchors lead story) opens the reel in <2s with frame-0 algorithmic-decision-engine catnip
- **Hooks 2 + 3** are *teaser-trailers for upcoming items* — they preview celebrities/topics that appear later in the numbered body, building anticipation
- **Stingers** between hook moments are mini pattern-interrupts — red all-caps caption + visual cut — like editorial commas

**Why it lands (decoded):**
1. Triple-stacked recognition (3 celebrities in 8 seconds) saturates face-recognition pathways, spiking dwell time across all three windows
2. Multi-instance flex (4 Sundars) signals "this reel was made with Sora-2 quality production" — credibility stack
3. Numbered countdown structure rewards completion ("did I see all 10?") — completion-rate is the algorithmic gate
4. Avatar small + neutral throughout = the visual budget goes to the hook moments, not the talking head

**Caption typography:**
- White Helvetica/Inter sentence-case with **yellow-highlighted punch word** per caption (e.g. "most powerful AI model" in yellow)
- Red all-caps for stingers (NO WAIT!!! / WAIT WHAT!!!)
- Numbered headers ("5 — GROK 4.1") in yellow on dark backdrop

**Production tools (decoded reality):**
- Hook 1 single-celebrity: harvested real press photo + cinematic processing (heavy contrast first 500ms)
- Hooks 2-3 multi-instance: generated via Sora 2 (cameo permission) OR via Gemini Nano Banana Pro with `refs[]` + descriptive identity
- Body items: product UI screen-recordings + CGI demo clips harvested from announcement tweets

**Steal:**
1. **Adopt the multi-hook teaser-trailer structure** for any topic that has 2+ company protagonists (acquisition rumors, comparison/benchmark news, ecosystem-wide trend takes)
2. **Use the red-stinger transitions** as pattern-interrupts between hook windows
3. **Numbered countdown body** rewards completion-rate (TikTok's primary algorithmic signal in 2026)
4. **Single-celebrity hook + cinematic processing first 500ms** for any single-topic reel — see AA.18 in hook-archetypes.md for full template

**This pattern was the seed for the Petdex reel's hook decision** — single-topic adaptation: skip the multi-celebrity teaser-trailer (only OpenAI is the protagonist), apply the multi-instance Sora-cameo flex (50 Sams in stadium seats, AA.19) as the single hook, drop the numbered-countdown body in favor of an 8-beat ironic-deadpan structure.

## §GG. Synthesis — patterns ALL viral 2026 AI reels share

1. **Hook lands on or before t=1.5s.** Tool-name + "going viral / crazy viral / blew through Turing Test" verbs lock the slot.
2. **Information-gap × Specificity.** "10 wild examples" beats "tutorial." Number quantifies the gap. "Wild / insane / crazy" is structurally identical across creators (in hooks, when wrapped in specificity).
3. **Tool-name in the hook.** Sora 2, Seedance, Nano Banana, Flux Kontext, Wan 2.2, Claude Code, MCP — naming gates SEO + routes to interest cohorts.
4. **Caption emphasis: single-word, single-color.** Yellow Helvetica Bold or white-with-yellow highlight is dominant. Multi-word emphasis underperforms.
5. **Cut cadence: 8-12 cuts/30s** for compilation reels; 1-2 cuts for "wow-clip" reels. Dead zone is 4-6 cuts.
6. **Music or silence — no in-between.** Either zero music (tool demos, photoreal) or cinematic trailer bed. Mid-volume lo-fi is dead.
7. **Hard CTA in last 3s.** "Follow for daily AI" (compounding) or keyword-comment funnel ("comment TOOL").

### GG.1 Patterns that DIFFER by sub-niche

| Sub-niche | Format | Length | Hook | Caption | Music |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| News drops (Rowan, Min Choi) | Avatar or compilation | 30s | "today/just dropped" | yellow bold | minimal/none |
| Tutorial (Justine) | Face-cam + screen-rec | 45-60s | "how it works" | rounded sans | none during workflow |
| Hot-take cultural | Phone-feed POV / static | 25s | observation pivot | sentence-case | NONE — silence is style |
| Demo (Sora-cameo, Higgsfield) | Single uncut clip | 8-12s | image-IS-the-hook | none on-video / 3-word delayed | cinematic trailer |
| Dev-flex (terminal/agent) | Screen-rec quadrant | 50s | era-metaphor | monospace | foley over music |

### GG.2 EMERGING in Q2 2026

- **Single-uncut clips** (anti-edit). 8-12s, zero cuts, cinematic music. Highest completion-rate format.
- **Avatar news reels** at 3-5×/day cadence (Rowan model). Volume is the moat.
- **Trend-audio + AI-tool mashup** (World Stop, Did I Miss Anything?). Riding non-AI trends to smuggle AI into general feeds.
- **Anti-design serif lowercase captions** for "premium" image-model demos. Refusing TikTok-bro aesthetic to signal taste.
- **Quadrant/multi-pane screen-rec** for agent demos. Status signal.
- **Sarcastic-deadpan captions** paired with genuinely impressive clips. Justine pioneered; spreading.
- **Topic-anchored AI wire-photos** (the AA.16 archetype — "what if Sundar/Altman/Musk got caught doing X" hook). Borrowed relevance from breaking news + 600ms photo-trust window before AI registers. Distinct from "100% AI" demo reels because AI is invisible — topic is the hook, AI is just the printer.

### GG.3 DECLINING / overdone (the "viral tax")

- **"Wait for it…"** Dead. -23% 3s-hold vs 18 months ago.
- **"This AI tool will change your life"** Dead. Vague benefit-promise no longer converts.
- **Heavy CapCut beat-cut edits with stock whooshes between every cut.** Algorithmically punished as "TikTok-bro."
- **Generic stock-footage AI reels.** When 7-Best-AI-Tools Reel #4173 looks identical, completion drops.
- **Animated emoji explosions in captions.** Abandoned by top tier.
- **"Stop scrolling"** as opener — flagged as low-effort. Works only when paired with genuinely contrarian frame.

### GG.4 Concrete recs for @aisimplified

1. **Adopt the Min Choi "10 wild examples" carousel** weekly. Pick model of the week, generate 8-10 clips, hard-cut every 3.6s, yellow Helvetica caption.
2. **Steal the Justine "recipe creator" position** for India-specific AI workflows. First creator with working recipe owns search.

8. **Caption emphasis: yellow Helvetica Bold, 1-2 words max per highlight.** Anything else is amateur.
9. **CTA: rotate keyword-comment funnel and "follow for daily AI" weekly.** Don't stack both.
